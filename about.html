Who is he?

Born in Colombia and raised in Puerto Rico, David moved to the U.S. to seek out a better college education. He attended the University of Central Florida, from which he graduated in 2014 with a Bachelor's in Computer Science. He knew, however, that that would not be enough for him. He knew he wanted to code programs and create websites, but he also wanted to give them elegant designs, because it's not just WHAT you say, it's also HOW you say it. With that in mind, he decided to seek out a Graphic/Interactive Design degree from Valencia College from where he's currently on his way to becoming a great designer.

TL;DR:
David Hernandez is the programmer/graphic designer that you are looking for. If you have a project, may it be a website, mobile app, or logo, in mind and you're and you are looking for a designer that can help you bring it to life then look no further, you have found him.
